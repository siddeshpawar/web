Design and Evaluation of StrongVPN: A Pure Post-Quantum VPN Architecture

Published research paper presenting StrongVPN, a novel VPN architecture designed with pure post-quantum cryptographic algorithms. This work addresses the critical challenge of securing VPN communications against future quantum computing threats by implementing NIST-standardized post-quantum algorithms throughout the entire VPN stack.

Key Contributions:

  • Complete post-quantum VPN architecture design and implementation
  • Integration of quantum-resistant key encapsulation mechanisms (KEMs) and digital signatures
  • Comprehensive performance evaluation comparing classical and PQC implementations
  • Security analysis demonstrating resistance to quantum attacks
  • Practical deployment considerations and migration strategies
